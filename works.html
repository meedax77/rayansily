Realestate Mangment System

The Real Estate Management System (REMS) is a user-friendly web application designed for efficient real estate property management. With secure user authentication and role-based access, REMS enables users to manage customers, buildings, units, and contracts seamlessly. The system supports CRUD operations for easy addition, modification, and deletion of records, ensuring an up-to-date and dynamic database. Key features include transaction history tracking, reporting tools, notification alerts, and robust security measures. REMS is a comprehensive solution for real estate professionals, providing transparency, analytics, and streamlined operations throughout the entire real estate transaction lifecycle.

Ghaith alshifa Medical complix webapp

The Ghaith Alshifa Medical WebApp is a user-friendly platform allowing visitors to access comprehensive details about the clinic, make appointments with doctors, and view clinic information. The appointment system notifies the reception for coordination. Employees can sign in to access and manage their documents. This integrated solution enhances patient experience and streamlines internal operations for medical professionals.
